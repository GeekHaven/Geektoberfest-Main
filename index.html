About GeektoberFest

GeektoberFest’20 is a 20 day long open source event running in parallel with the Hacktober fest. We have set up some beginner friendly repositories to help you begin your journey in the daunting world of open source and help you take your first step in your development career.

This roller coaster ride will begin at 12 midnight on 10th Oct and will keep you entertained till 12 midnight on 31st October.

To encourage the budding developers all participants with a minimum 4 valid Pull requests would be eligible for the goodies from Hacktoberfest in addition to that GeekHaven would also be giving away prizes to the best performers.
